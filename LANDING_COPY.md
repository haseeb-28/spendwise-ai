## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free? What's the catch?**
A: Completely free. No login, no credit card, no trial period. SpendWise is a free tool built by Credex. If your audit shows significant savings, we'll mention Credex credits as one option — but there's no obligation and no upsell pressure.

**Q: How accurate are the savings estimates?**
A: Every recommendation cites the specific pricing difference and the reasoning. We don't estimate vague percentages — we calculate exact savings based on official vendor pricing (sourced and dated in our pricing data). If a number seems off, check the vendor's pricing page and let us know.

**Q: Do you store my spend data?**
A: Your audit results are stored so you can share them via a unique URL. The public version strips your email and company name — only tool names and savings numbers are visible. You can delete your data by emailing us.

**Q: What tools does SpendWise cover?**
A: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API, OpenAI API, Gemini, and Windsurf. We update pricing every week and add tools based on demand.

**Q: What if my spend is already optimized?**
A: We'll tell you honestly. "You're spending well" is a valid and useful output — it means you can stop second-guessing your AI budget. You can also sign up to be notified when new optimizations apply to your stack.
